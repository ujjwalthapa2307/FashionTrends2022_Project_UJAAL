WHAT IS FASHION SPY??

FASHION SPY is a technological solution that allows us to fully understand fashion products, consumers, and market trends by analyzing past and present fashion data.

Based on multiple factors, our machine learning algorithm would provide customers and businesses with predictive data on trends and goods. Our predictive analytics model will be used to measure demand and trends based on growth, volume, adoption pace, etc.

Additionally, our solution would offer fashion trends related to eco-friendly products and recommendations related to them.
